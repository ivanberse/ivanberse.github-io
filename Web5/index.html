Welcome to the 2016 collection from 3M

What does fashion mean? In contrast to clothing, which is functional and only changes gradually, fashion thrives on novelty and change. Fashion is often seen as a ‘value’ added to clothes to make them desirable to consumers. It is a contradiction: homogenizing, encouraging everyone to dress in the same way, but also it stands for individuality. Not merely about clothes, fashion is a vibrant form of visual and material culture that plays an important role in social and cultural life. It is a major economic force.
